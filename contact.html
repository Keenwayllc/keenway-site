Contact Keenway

Need a pickup and delivery in Los Angeles County? Send the details below and you will get a clear, direct answer, owner to customer. Email support@gokeenway.com or book a pickup & delivery to get started.

Best use cases

Keenway is a strong fit for same-day business deliveries, scheduled recurring routes, auto parts runs, medical and lab pickups, retail orders, building materials and paint, and furniture or bulky items that need a cargo van. If a courier turned it down or a car is too small, it is worth sending over.

What to send before booking

To get a fast, accurate answer, include these details up front:

  • Pickup address, where the item is now.
  • Delivery address, where it needs to go.
  • Item details, what it is, rough size and weight, and how many pieces.
  • Timing, same-day or a scheduled pickup window.
  • Access notes, gate codes, dock or floor, parking limits, or anyone to ask for on arrival.
