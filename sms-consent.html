SMS Consent

How Keenwaysm uses text messaging — and how to control your preferences.

Opt-In Agreement

By submitting your phone number through a Keenway form, booking page, customer account, or delivery request, you agree to receive text messages from Keenwaysm and Keenway LLC.

Message Types

These messages may include:

  • Delivery confirmations
  • Pickup and drop-off updates
  • Driver or dispatch notifications
  • Tracking updates
  • Customer support messages
  • Account or service-related notifications

Message frequency varies based on your delivery activity.

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out & Help

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from text messages.

Reply HELP for assistance.

Contact & Support

For support, contact us at support@gokeenway.com.
